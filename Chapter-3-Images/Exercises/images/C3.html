Sylvanian Families

First introduced in 1985 in Japan, Sylvanian Families are minimalist-looking animal figurines that are, according to the brand, timeless and classic toys that “promote wholesome family values”. The toys are best known for the “aesthetically pleasing” cottagecore attire they wear while they act out dramatic adult scenarios — like paying bills and getting divorced.
